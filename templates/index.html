Importance of Customer Churn in the Telecom Sector

Customer churn is a crucial metric in the telecom industry. It measures the rate at which customers stop using the service. Reducing churn is vital for telecom companies as it leads to increased revenue, customer retention, and a competitive advantage in the industry.

About the Classifier

This classifier is designed to predict customer churn based on multiple factors, including customer demographics, service usage, and payment history. By understanding which customers are likely to churn, telecom providers can take proactive steps to retain them.
